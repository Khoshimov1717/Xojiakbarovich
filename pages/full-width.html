About

An online magazine dedicated to modern design, Xojiakbarovich offers what’s new in art, architecture, interior design, furniture and decor, fashion and technology.

Design Milk has been featured in the Los Angeles Times, Time Out New York, The San Francisco Chronicle, was one of the Google Reader Engineers’ Staff Picks and has been noted on Twitter’s “Who to Follow” list of influencers in Art and Design.

It’s also been featured in books, and is read by many entertainers, hundreds of retailers, magazine editors, and HGTV hosts and designers.
